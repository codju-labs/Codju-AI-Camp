# AI Creator Camp by Codju
**Domain:** codju.com
**Target Audience:** Students in Grades 6–10 (ages 11–16)
**Dates:** June 22 – 28, 2025
**Format:** Live Online (Zoom), 45-minute daily sessions
**Cost / Refund:** 100% Risk-Free Guarantee (Cancel anytime before Day 1 for full refund)

## Program Description
AI Creator Camp by Codju is an immersive 7-day online program designed to teach young students the fundamentals and creative applications of Artificial Intelligence. Rather than teaching students to fear AI, the camp focuses on empowering them to lead with it through hands-on, project-based learning. Students learn to use modern AI tools to build real-world projects such as websites, apps, artwork, and videos.

## Key Outcomes & Deliverables
- **8 Real Projects:** Students will build practical projects during the camp.
- **15+ AI Tools Taught:** Students get hands-on experience with tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Canva AI, Leonardo AI, CapCut AI, HeyGen, Suno, Gamma, NotebookLM, Notion AI, Scratch, Miro AI, Wix AI, Lovable, Glide, and Replit AI.
- **Certificate of Completion:** A verified certificate is awarded to students who complete the camp.
- **12 Months of Support:** Enrolled students receive a full year of AI support, including monthly update sessions, access to future live batches, and an AI mentor hotline.
- **Demo Day:** The camp concludes with a team hackathon and a live Demo Day presentation.

## Daily Curriculum
- **Day 1: Prompt Engineering:** Learn the mechanics of talking to AI, drafting research outlines, and breaking down complex topics using tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.
- **Day 2: Creativity with AI:** Generate visual art, stories, and videos using Leonardo AI, Midjourney, Suno, CapCut, and HeyGen.
- **Day 3: AI for Learning:** Create a personal AI study dashboard, including notes, summaries, and quizzes using NotebookLM, Notion AI, and Gamma.
- **Day 4: Build a Website:** Learn computational thinking and build a real website from scratch using Canva Websites, Wix AI, and Scratch.
- **Day 5: Build an App:** Turn ideas into a working mini app using AI-assisted development tools like Lovable, Glide, Replit AI, and Miro AI.
- **Day 6: AI Agents & Automation:** Learn automation fundamentals, custom GPTs, and connect tools to build workflows using Make.com, ChatGPT, and ElevenLabs.
- **Day 7: Hackathon + Demo Day:** Collaborate in teams, present projects, and showcase the final outcomes to mentors and parents.
