The Learning Path

7-Day Learning Plan

Each day builds on the last. By Day 7, students present real projects they built themselves.

Day 1

Prompt Engineering

  • How AI works in daily life
  • Prompt structures
  • Creative, productivity & research prompting
  • AI debates & quizzes
Covering AI Tools: ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity
Day 2

Creativity with AI

  • AI image generation
  • Poster design
  • AI storytelling
  • Video, avatars & voiceovers
  • AI music
Covering AI Tools: Canva AI, Ideogram, Suno, CapCut AI
Day 3

AI-Assisted Learning

  • AI note-making & summaries
  • Quiz & flashcard generation
  • Smart, ethical AI study habits
  • Presentation design
Covering AI Tools: Gamma, NotebookLM, Gemini, ChatGPT
Day 4

Computational Thinking + Build a Website

  • Problem identification
  • Breaking problems into steps
  • Project planning
  • Logic with Scratch
  • AI website building
Covering AI Tools: Scratch, Lovable, Canva Websites
Day 5

Entrepreneurial Thinking + Build an App

  • Startup and product thinking
  • Branding and naming
  • Pitch craft
  • Prompt-to-app building (vibe coding)
Covering AI Tools: Lovable, Gamma, Canva AI
Day 6

AI Agents & Automation

  • What AI agents are
  • Automation and workflows
  • Task planning and execution
  • AI assistants for daily life
Covering AI Tools: Make.com, ChatGPT, ElevenLabs
Day 7

Hackathon + Demo Day

  • Final project showcase
  • Hackathon presentation
  • Demo Day
  • Covering tools from the entire week
Covering AI Tools: Covering all tools from the week
